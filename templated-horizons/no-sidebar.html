Down to a Science

Using smart projections that don't take the fun out of making your own picks

Fantasy football. It's huge. It means everything to you. Why should you make your decisions by your "research" that bareley scrapes the surface or your gut? We have developed a better way to determine which guys to give the nod and play or sit each week. Our personally tailored advice is split into two main components: standard league play (think ESPN or CBS Sports) and salary-style (DraftKings and FanDuel).

We provide to you the most cost effective roster that maximizes your points in salary-style leagues. However, if you think that you know one or more players that are going to have a breakout week who didn't make our list, you can substitute him in, and our algorithms will adjust accordingly. The roster will adjust itself to your wants and deliver a roster that will maximize your points for the best price with those players involved. You can use our picks for all your players, you can use it for just one, or anything in between, we use advanced analytics to find the most cost effective players that give you the best chance of winning.

For standard league play, we use a combination of sources to gather projections for each player on your team, and recommend a starting lineup. Right now, every website has their own projections (most of which are often very off come Sunday), however, ours have shown significantly better accuracy. If there's a player on the waiver wire that will outperform one of your starters, we make sure you know. We provide all the insight needed with the most advanced numbers to make sure you are in the position to get the W every single week.
